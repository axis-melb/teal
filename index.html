Professional Integrity

Role-Segregation Model

We strictly adhere to the Victorian Building Professional Code of Conduct regarding professional independence. To provide our clients with the most robust and ethical service, we operate under a clear segregation model:

Strategic Consulting

Where appointed for pre-design/planning advice, we act as dedicated consultants. Statutory permit issuance is facilitated through an independent third-party Surveyor.

Statutory Surveying (RBS)

When acting as the Relevant Building Surveyor (RBS), we maintain strict statutory control and do not provide conflicting design or planning consultancy.
